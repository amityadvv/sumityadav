Projects

IoT Based Smart Agriculture System

During my college years, I led a team in developing an IoT-based agriculture system aimed at revolutionizing traditional farming methods.

The primary objective was to enhance efficiency, productivity, and sustainability in agriculture through the integration of IoT components such as sensors, actuators, and connectivity.

  • Designed a modular system adaptable to various crop types, growth stages, and environmental conditions.
  • Implemented a network of sensors to collect real-time data on temperature, humidity, soil moisture, and light levels.
  • Utilized IoT protocols to transmit data to a central hub for analysis and decision-making.
  • Developed intelligent algorithms for automated irrigation based on soil moisture levels, reducing water waste and ensuring precise watering.
  • Conducted extensive field trials with local farmers to validate system effectiveness, resulting in increased crop yields and improved resource management.

This project demonstrated the transformative potential of IoT and data analytics in modernizing agriculture practices, providing farmers with actionable insights and predictive capabilities for sustainable farming.
